Stardew Valley

Stardew Valley is my favorite video game. It is a very calming and has really nice art to look at. The music is also great, and matches the vibe of the game very well.

It has tons of content, keeping you entertained for hours on just one game. Due to it being marketed as a farming game, many think that is the only thing you can do in Stardew Valley. However, this is incorrect. There are aspects of combat, fishing, mining, and more, giving you tons of things to do in a day.

Although the game is relatively pricey at around $15, I believe it is more than worth it, as I can gauranteed that you will get more than $15 worth of content and fun from this game.
